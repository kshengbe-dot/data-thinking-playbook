Data Thinking

How I Approach Data

My approach to data is not tool-first — it is problem-first. I focus on understanding the question, preparing data correctly, and turning information into decisions.

STEP 01

Start With the Question

Every analysis starts with understanding the real problem. I define what needs to be answered before touching data.

STEP 02

Clean and Prepare Data

I clean, structure, and transform data to ensure it is accurate and usable for analysis.

STEP 03

Explore Patterns

I analyze trends, distributions, and relationships to uncover meaningful patterns.

STEP 04

Extract Insights

I turn raw findings into insights that explain what is happening and why.

STEP 05

Communicate Clearly

I present results using dashboards and visuals so both technical and non-technical audiences can understand.

STEP 06

Drive Decisions

The final goal is impact — helping people make better decisions using data.

What this shows:

I don’t just analyze data — I structure problems, think critically, and turn information into action.
